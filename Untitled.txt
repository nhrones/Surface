
Right-click: on a .ts file.
Select "Open with" and then click "Choose another app".
Check the box that says "Always use this app to open .ts files".
Scroll down and select "Look for another app on this PC".
Navigate to the location of your TypeScript compiler (e.g., C:\Program Files\nodejs\node_modules\typescript\bin\tsc.exe).
Select "tsc.exe" and click "Open".
